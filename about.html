Our Story

Built in Ontario. Reaching 50+ Countries.

VaultVelocityAuto is a Francisco Holdings Inc. venture, founded in Ontario, Canada. We saw an industry where the finest collector vehicles were being sold through word of mouth, regional dealers, and opaque private networks — inaccessible to the majority of serious buyers worldwide.

We changed that. By combining a commission-based model (we only make money when you do), rigorous Vault Certified inspections, escrow-protected transactions, and a globally reaching digital marketplace, we've made the world's finest cars available to the world's most discerning buyers — wherever they are.

Our platform is built around three principles: transparency (every car's history is documented and published), trust (every transaction is escrow-protected and every seller is verified), and reach (your vehicle reaches 50,000+ qualified collectors in 50+ countries, not just your local market).

We are not a dealer. We carry no inventory. We are a pure marketplace — commission-based, seller-aligned, and buyer-protected. When a legend changes hands through VaultVelocityAuto, everyone wins.

Trust & Safety

How We Protect Every Transaction

🔍
Seller Verification
Every seller on VaultVelocityAuto is identity-verified before their listing goes live. We confirm ownership of the vehicle and review the title before an auction opens.
  • Government ID verification
  • Title ownership confirmation
  • Phone and email verification
  • Fraud pattern screening
🛡️
Buyer Protection Escrow
Buyers never send payment directly to sellers. All funds are held in escrow by VaultVelocityAuto until delivery is confirmed and the buyer's inspection period clears.
  • Funds held until delivery confirmed
  • 5-day inspection period included
  • Full refund if vehicle misrepresented
  • Dispute resolution guarantee
✓
Vault Certified Program
Our proprietary 150-point inspection program verifies every vehicle before it's listed as Vault Certified — covering mechanical, cosmetic, frame, numbers matching, and title.
  • 150-point mechanical inspection
  • Numbers matching verification
  • Frame and chassis assessment
  • Full photographic documentation
Vault Certified

The Most Rigorous Inspection in Collector Cars

Every Vault Certified badge means an independent inspector has physically assessed the vehicle against our 150-point checklist. Not a checklist sellers fill out. A real inspection by a real expert.

01
Physical Inspection Arranged
Once a seller submits their vehicle, we dispatch an independent Vault Certified inspector to the vehicle's location. The seller cannot choose the inspector.
02
150-Point Assessment
Our inspector works through a standardized 150-point checklist: engine, drivetrain, chassis, body, interior, electrical, title history, and numbers matching.
03
Full Documentation Package
Every inspection generates a timestamped photo library, a written report, and a summary score. This full package is published on the vehicle's auction page for all buyers to review.
04
Vault Certified Badge Issued
Once the vehicle passes our standards, the Vault Certified badge is applied to the listing. This badge is our guarantee — backed by the buyer protection escrow program.

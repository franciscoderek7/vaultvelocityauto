FAQ
Common Questions
Every vehicle sold through VaultVelocityAuto is covered by our Vault Certified buyer protection. Funds are held in escrow until the buyer receives and accepts the vehicle. If the vehicle is materially misrepresented, buyers are entitled to a full refund. Vault Certified inspections are performed on all Premium and Concierge listings before auction.
VaultVelocityAuto charges sellers a 5% commission on the final sale price, capped at $25,000 on very high-value vehicles. There is no buyer's premium. The success fee is only charged when your vehicle sells — if it doesn't sell, you pay nothing beyond the listing fee. Premium listings have a reduced 4% fee; Concierge listings are 3%.

Buyer Protection

🛡️ Vault Certified Guarantee: Every vehicle sold through VaultVelocityAuto is covered by our escrow protection. Funds are not released to sellers until buyers confirm receipt and acceptance of the vehicle.

What Vault Certified Covers

  • Material misrepresentation of vehicle condition, mileage, or history
  • Vehicle not matching listing description at time of delivery
  • Title defects that prevent legal transfer of ownership
  • Non-delivery of a paid vehicle

Claim Process

If you believe a vehicle has been materially misrepresented, you must notify us within 48 hours of delivery. Provide photographic evidence and a written description of the discrepancy. We will investigate and mediate within 5 business days. If the claim is validated, your funds are returned in full.

What Buyer Protection Does Not Cover

  • Pre-existing conditions disclosed in the listing
  • Normal wear for the vehicle's age and mileage
  • Issues discovered after the 48-hour inspection window
  • Import duties, taxes, or compliance costs in your jurisdiction
  • Mechanical issues that develop after acceptance

Accuracy of Listing

You represent that all information in your listing is accurate and complete to the best of your knowledge. Material omissions are treated the same as misrepresentations. Sellers are responsible for disclosing all known defects, accidents, modifications, and title encumbrances.

Title and Authority

You warrant that you are the legal owner or authorized agent for the vehicle, that the title is clear of liens (or that any liens will be paid from sale proceeds), and that you have the legal right to sell the vehicle.

Reserve Price

Your reserve price is the minimum acceptable bid. If bidding does not reach your reserve, the vehicle does not sell and your listing fee is non-refundable. If bidding exceeds your reserve, you are obligated to complete the sale at the winning bid price.

The Process

How VaultVelocityAuto Works

1

Seller Lists

Choose your listing tier — Basic ($99), Premium ($299), or Concierge ($999). Set your reserve. We handle the Vault Certified inspection, photography, and listing creation.

2

Buyers Bid Live

Your vehicle reaches our network of 50,000+ qualified collectors globally. Live bidding, real-time countdowns, and complete transparency — every bid verified, every bidder authenticated.

3

We Handle the Rest

On a successful sale, we collect a 5% success fee. We coordinate escrow, title transfer, enclosed transport, and financing for the buyer. You get paid. We handle everything.
